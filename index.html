CAN I EAT IT?

Eating on the train

"Can I eat it?" Yes, actually you CAN EAT everything in this world. But then there are some places that vorbidden you from eating. In the railtrain!

I've done a lot of research, and as far as I know, there aren't any laws against eating on trains. It's more of a common courtesy thing, where people understand each other and don't cause any trouble by eating in public.

That's exactly why I made this project: "Can i eat it?" to remind people, not to behave too rudely on train. I feel really awkward when someone on the train is eating something that smells a bit.

I scan different kinds of food or collect 3D models in SketchLab, then put them on the floor of the train. I want to make you feel like you're walking through a party of food, but on a train!
